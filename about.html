What is this

Mapping Memphis is an interactive map that gives historical contextualization of the various parts of memphis. Here, we have contextualized two areas as a demo of the potential a contextualized mapping of the city has. The purpose of the neighborhood map is be to create a database that contains demographic information for social science or digital humanities research (i.e. race, age, life span, cause of death, spikes in mortality rates, outbreaks and track a significant number of outbreaks. etc.). In conjunction, there is a Story Board presentation of two neighborhoods, Orange Mound and Hyde Park, that can be used as preliminary trials in the inception of the coding. Socioeconomic data could also be taken in, such as upward mobility, average occupation and income. Ideally, various statistics would be added to the map so that the maximum amount of relevant research aids could be established before inserting more neighborhoods into the program.
