Hyde Park

Overview

Hyde Park is a neighborhood with a historically bustling and involved community. While Orange Mound is known to have boasted a community center that focused positive energy in the neighborhood, fostered a sense of place, and furthered feelings of ownership, the Hyde Park neighborhood is less known. Located in north Memphis, it begins at the Hyde Park St. and Chelsea Ave. intersection and is situated in the Hollywood community. Though relatively obscure presently, Hyde Park was an area of activity, often featured in the Memphis World newspaper for their conscious moves towards improving established community programs and building up a critical mass of involved members to coordinate and support the growth.
